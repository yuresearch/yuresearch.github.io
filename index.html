About

We are a group of engineers with diverse and complementary backgrounds, including electrical engineering, biomedical engineering, materials science, chemistry, and mechanical engineering. We collaborate in teams to create new devices and technologies while understanding fundamental mechanisms to address grand challenges in healthcare, medicine, robotics, and more.

Our research is driven by combined efforts in multi-scale manufacturing technologies, materials engineering, mechanical design, and electronics and sensors development.
